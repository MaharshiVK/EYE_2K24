EYE Electrify Your Education is a National Level Technical Symposium organized by the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering JNTUA CEA. It gives a chance to the students across the nation to explore their talent and win exciting prizes. This is a One-Day Technical symposium that includes various events such as Workshops, Circuit Hunt, Project Expo, Paper presentations and Quiz.
